FAYE

I'm Rokheyatou "Roxie" Faye, a mechanical engineer and visual artist working at the intersection of design, systems, and storytelling.

My practice is shaped by a background that spans fabrication, studio art, and cultural institutions, including time as a cultural mediation intern at the Museum of Black Civilizations in Dakar, Senegal. Moving between a design lab and a studio has taught me to hold space for analytical thinking and creative intuition.

What drives me is the question of how things are made and what they mean. I build, prototype, and refine, but I'm equally interested in the cultural and material conditions that shape a design decision. My work is an ongoing attempt to examine how form, function, and meaning coexist.
